FAQ

Common Questions

AttentionX supports all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM. Files can be up to 2GB. For best results, we recommend MP4 with H.264 encoding at 1080p or higher.

Our pipeline combines three signals: (1) Audio energy analysis using Librosa detects where the speaker's voice is most passionate, (2) Sentiment analysis on the Whisper transcript identifies emotionally charged language, and (3) Gemini 1.5 Flash synthesizes the full context to score each moment for shareability.

A 60-minute video typically takes 8–12 minutes end-to-end: ~2 min for transcription, ~3 min for AI analysis, ~4 min for video rendering and export. Clips are processed in parallel to minimize total time.

Output clips are exported at 1080×1920 (9:16) — the standard for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Captions are rendered at full resolution and positioned in the lower third for optimal mobile readability.
