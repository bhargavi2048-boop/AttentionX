Our Solution

The AttentionX
Engine

GenAI + Multimodal models working together to repurpose content automatically.

AttentionX is a platform where users upload long-form videos, and the app uses GenAI and Multimodal models to automatically extract, reformat, and caption the most impactful moments — turning one session into a week's worth of viral content.

01
🧠

Emotional Peak Detection

Our AI analyzes the full video transcript using Google Gemini 1.5 Flash with its massive 1M+ token context window. Combined with Librosa for audio loudness analysis, the system pinpoints exact moments where the speaker is most passionate, energetic, or insightful.

  • Audio spike detection for vocal energy mapping
  • Sentiment analysis on transcript text
  • Keyword clustering for topic significance
  • Outputs precise timestamps for clip extraction
Gemini 1.5 Flash Librosa NLP
02
📱

Smart Crop to Vertical

Using MediaPipe's Face Detection model, AttentionX tracks the speaker's face frame-by-frame. The crop window dynamically adjusts to keep the speaker perfectly centered, then MoviePy exports a crisp 9:16 vertical clip ready for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts.

  • Real-time face detection and tracking
  • Dynamic crop window adjustment
  • Smooth pan to follow movement
  • Exports at 1080×1920 resolution
MediaPipe MoviePy OpenCV
03
✍️

Dynamic Caption Generation

OpenAI Whisper transcribes the audio with high-accuracy timestamps. The captions are then styled as high-contrast, animated word-by-word overlays. Additionally, Gemini generates catchy "hook" headlines — the first line that stops the scroll.

  • Word-level timestamp accuracy from Whisper
  • Karaoke-style animated captions
  • High-contrast design for silent viewing
  • AI-generated scroll-stopping hooks
OpenAI Whisper Gemini MoviePy
🚀

The Result

A single 60-minute mentorship session becomes 7+ shareable vertical clips, each with perfect framing, dynamic captions, and a viral hook — in minutes, not days.
