Problem Statement

The Attention Crisis
in Modern Content

Valuable wisdom is buried in long-form video. Modern audiences can't find it.

Mentors, educators, and creators produce hours of high-value, long-form video content — lectures, podcasts, workshops. However, modern audiences consume information in 60-second bursts. Valuable "golden nuggets" of wisdom are often buried in 60-minute videos, making them inaccessible to the average viewer.

⏱

The Attention Gap

The average human attention span for online video has dropped to under 8 seconds. Long-form content simply doesn't compete in a world of infinite scroll.

8 sec avg attention
🔍

Buried Golden Nuggets

Sifting through hours of footage manually to find "viral" or high-impact moments is time-consuming, expensive, and often results in missed opportunities.

60+ hrs wasted/month
📐

Wrong Format, Wrong Platform

Converting horizontal 16:9 video to vertical 9:16 while keeping the speaker centered is a technical challenge most creators don't have the tools for.

70% posted wrong ratio
📝

Caption Bottleneck

Manually transcribing and timing "karaoke-style" captions to keep viewers engaged adds days to the publishing pipeline for every video.

3+ days per video
"The best content in the world doesn't matter if it's buried inside a 60-minute video nobody will watch."
— The Content Paradox of 2026
